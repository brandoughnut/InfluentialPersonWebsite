About LeBron James
LeBron Raymone James Sr. who was born December 30, 1984, is an American professional basketball player who is currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers of the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is widely recognized as one of the greatest players in the history of the sport and has competed in 10 NBA Finals (with eight consecutive appearances from 2011 to 2018), winning four NBA championships. He has many accolades, one of which is becoming the all time leading scorer of the NBA.
