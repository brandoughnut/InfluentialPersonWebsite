Influence
Maintaining Health
LeBron is currently 39 years old, and is in his 21st season of the NBA. The fact that he is able to consistently play at a high level for so long proves that he focuses on his fitness and health. LeBron inspires me to continue exercising and working out so that I can maintain my health as I get older.
Performing Under Pressure
LeBron was drafted to the NBA straight after he completed high school. He was a prospect who many were anticipating. Although LeBron had a lot of pressure on him to perform, he delivered such as being the youngest player to receive Rookie of the Year. Personally, I crack a lot under pressure because the thoughts get to me, but I strive to strengthen my mental, and live with the outcome.
