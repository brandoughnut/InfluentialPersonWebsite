Timeline
LeBron spent his first seven seasons in the NBA with the Cleveland Cavaliers. A leader on and off the floor, LeBron helped lead the Cavs to three postseason appearances.
After exercising his free agency for the first time in his career, James joined the Miami Heat in the 2010-11 season. The team won back-to-back championships in 2012 and 2013 with LeBron earning Finals MVP awards in each campaign.
In July of 2014, LeBron memorably announced he would return to the Cleveland Cavaliers on a mission to deliver his home state a title. LeBron led the Cavaliers back from a 3-1 deficit to win the franchise’s first championship.
